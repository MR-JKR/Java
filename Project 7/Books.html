Learning and Knowledge

Learning is the process of acquiring knowledge, while knowledge is the information, skills, and facts that are acquired through study, experience, or education. Learning is a dynamic process that can occur throughout a person's life, and it involves actively engaging with new information, abilities, or understanding. Knowledge, on the other hand, involves understanding, comprehension, and mastery of information.
